A 3D printed model of Grogu

This 3D printed model of Grogu from the Star Wars series "The Mandalorian", was printed over a couple of days. It was then painted and dressed and even given a slight tuft of hair and glossed eyes for extra detail.
All the parts are fully articulated, so he can sit and be moved into any pose.
